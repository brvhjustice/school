Welcome to the Justice Senior High School website. Whetther you are already part of the Justice SHS or are browsing to learn more about our school, we hope your exploration leads you to useful information and insight into who we are. Our board and balanced program is grounded in our mission and vision of being a Learning Focused, Leading and Connected school. We strive to integrate the IB Learner Profile attributes and the values of trustworthiness, respect, responsibility, fairness,and citizenship into all areas of the school.

Our website is rich source of information about the school, and within its pages you will learn how we have built a reputation as one of the top international schools on the continent through our commitment to:

  • Inspiring students to achieve the highest standards of intellectual and personal development through a stimulating and comprehensive program ar every level
  • Developing in each of our students a deep love of learning and a sustained desire to pursue personal passions.
  • Ensuring all students enjoy a diverse range of learning choices in currucular and co-curricular programs aimed at driving them towards achieving their goals in academics, arts, athletics and action in the community.
  • Embracing cultural diversity at JSHS, with around 950 students, representing more than 50 countries and religions, in one open and welcoming Justice Senior High School.
  • Offering a rich, relevant, and balanced education where passionate teachers strive to guide all our students to become successful, independent, and confident Justice Senior High School learners.

Together, the faculty and staff of JSHS offer the highest quality international education. As the Head of School, I am confident that our students - surrounded as they are by inspiring and caring adults - are constantly engaged in meaningful intellectual and community challenges.
