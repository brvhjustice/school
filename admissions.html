ADMISSIONS PROCESS

Data is submitted via the online application form hosted by Open Apply which is integrated on our website, hosted by 000webhostapp

Once Your child is admitted to J.S.H.S. you will receive further information about how we will use data that you shared with us and and we will seek the necessary consent from you in processing the data further. The data you share with the school will form part of your contractual agreement with the school. You have our assurance that once your child is admitted to JSHS, data will be securely store in our internal and external school management systems. Physical, electronic and procedural safeguards designed to help secure our internal systems and your personal information and to assist us in preventing unauthorized access, use, disclosure, loss, altertion and/or destruction of that information are in place at JSHS.

STEP 1- Starting to Apply Online

**with the submission of an admissions application form to JSHS, you agree to allow JSHS to use the personal data you provided through the JSHS student admissions system.

  • Click on Inquiry to create an account. Once you have done this, you will receive an email from the Admissions Office confirming your username and password.

  • Return to your account at anytime to complete the online application (ensure you have selected the appropriate school year of application).

Additional documents which you will need to have in PDF format for the application process include:

  • An active National Health Insurance card

  • Medical Forms (immunization, Health information and Examination forms)

  • Result slip from the previous Junior High School

  • Other assessments/evaluations/documentaion, etc... if applicable.

Additional information you will be asked to provide in the application process:

  • Online application fee of $200.00 required at the time of submission. Payment can be made with either a debit or credit card

  • The Confidential School Report will be sent through JSHS to the appropriate individual(s) at your ward's current school. Please have thier email contacts ready to enter when you get to that point during the online process.

Once you submit the online Application, you will receive a confirmation of receipt from Admission Office. You will have the option to print or save the completed application for your records.

Important Note
The Admissions Office must have a fully completed application, including payment of application fee, before starting to evalute your child's file for admission. Once the complete application is submitted, you will be able to check the status of your ward or students admission status.

STEP 2 - Admission Decision

As general practice, admission decisions are completed after the applicant has submitted a completed online applicaion. Once reviewed, the final admission decision will be conveyed to the parents by the Admissions Office.

STEP 3 - Enrollment

Once an admission letter and invoice has been received, you will have ten working days to pay the one time, non-refundable registration fee of USD $6500 to ensure your child's pplace at JSHS. If the fee is not received within the ten day period, your child will not be considered as enrolled.

Orientations

The Admissions Office conducts orientation programs for new families at the beginning of each new school year and at the beginning of second semester. Students who are admitted once the school year has started receive a customized orientation from the Admissions Office.
